How It Works
Dreidel: each player starts with 7 tokens and spins on their turn until one player controls everything in the pot.
Poker: both players get 5 cards, then each player may discard up to 3 cards one time before the showdown.
Winnings: both players buy in with the same wager, and the winner takes the whole pot. A poker tie splits it back evenly.
